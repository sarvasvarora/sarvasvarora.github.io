### How do I follow and get updates for this website?
The best way to follow and get updates is to subscribe to [RSS Feeds](https://sarvasvarora.me/feed.xml). There are a couple of tools and websites that help you get subscribed, such as [Feeder](https://feeder.co/) and its Google Chrome extension [RSS Feed Reader](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/rss-feed-reader/pnjaodmkngahhkoihejjehlcdlnohgmp?hl=en), or [Feedly](https://feedly.com/). I sometimes post links to new blog posts and articles on my [Twitter](https://twitter.com/sarvasvarora) as well, but there is no guarantee for that.
